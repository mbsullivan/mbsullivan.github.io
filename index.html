Approximate Computing

The speed and levels of integration of modern devices have risen to the point that arithmetic can be performed in hardware with high precision. While precise arithmetic is important for some applications, computing past the precision that applications require wastes power. Novel approximate methods of computation can save power and latency relative to covnentional arithmetic circuits. There remains plenty of interesting research into the design and application of approximate arithmetic units.
